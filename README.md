# 🥗 NutriPak — Pakistani Nutrition, Health Tracking & AI Diet Planning App

**🚀 A localized Flutter nutrition platform for Pakistani users, combining meal tracking, micronutrient analysis, health-condition-aware food guidance, AI diet planning, and nutritionist consultation.**

> **📌 Public Case Study Notice**
> This repository is a public case study and project showcase. Source code is private to protect academic/project ownership, API keys, Firebase configuration, user data, and implementation details.

---

## 📖 Overview

NutriPak is a mobile/web nutrition and health tracking application designed specifically for Pakistani users. It is inspired by apps like MyFitnessPal, but localized for Pakistani foods, Pakistani meal habits, common health concerns, micronutrient deficiencies, halal-friendly diet planning, and nutritionist consultation workflows.

The app helps users track daily meals, understand calorie and macro intake, analyze micronutrient gaps, receive food-based suggestions for nutritional deficiencies, generate AI-assisted diet plans, and connect with nutritionists for professional guidance.

Unlike a basic calorie counter, NutriPak focuses on **health-aware nutrition intelligence**. It does not simply tell users how many calories they consumed; it also looks at health conditions, nutritional concerns, micronutrients, and safer food choices.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Most popular calorie tracking apps are not built around Pakistani food culture.

Pakistani meals are difficult to track accurately because many users eat homemade foods, mixed dishes, and flexible serving sizes such as roti, rice, salan, biryani, karahi, daal, snacks, chai, and traditional meals. Generic apps often lack local food items, local serving patterns, and culturally relevant diet guidance.

Another major issue is that many people only track calories while ignoring micronutrients. Deficiencies in nutrients such as iron, vitamin D, calcium, vitamin B12, folate, zinc, and protein can contribute to fatigue, weakness, hair fall, poor skin health, low energy, and general wellness problems.

Users with diabetes, high blood pressure, cholesterol, obesity, anemia, PCOS, thyroid issues, digestive problems, kidney concerns, liver concerns, or heart-related conditions also need safer food guidance. A generic food tracker may show calories but may not explain whether a food choice is suitable for a user’s health profile.

Many users also need personalized diet guidance but cannot easily access a nutritionist, especially for routine diet planning and follow-up.

NutriPak was created to address these gaps with a localized, health-aware, and practical nutrition tracking experience.

## ✅ Solution

NutriPak provides a complete nutrition tracking flow for Pakistani users:

* 🇵🇰 Local Pakistani food database
* 🍽️ Meal logging with daily summaries

* 🧑‍⚕️ Personalized onboarding and nutrition target calculation

* 🥦 Food-based recommendations instead of medicine-first suggestions

* 👨‍⚕️ Nutritionist consultation and diet plan delivery flow

NutriPak’s core idea is simple:

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧑‍💼 A. Personalized Onboarding

NutriPak starts with a guided onboarding flow where users provide basic health and lifestyle information. The app uses this profile to estimate personalized calorie and macro targets.

Onboarding includes:

* 👤 Basic user profile
* 📏 Body metrics
* 🎯 Fitness goal
* 🏃 Activity level
* 🥗 Dietary preference
* 🩺 Health conditions
* 🧬 Nutritional concerns
* ✅ Personalized summary

This allows the app to provide more relevant nutrition guidance instead of using one-size-fits-all recommendations.

---

### 📊 B. Dashboard

The dashboard gives users a quick daily overview of their nutrition and health progress.

It includes:

* 🔥 Daily calorie summary

* 💧 Hydration tracking
* 🏃 Activity snapshot
* ⚖️ Weight indicator
* 🧬 Micronutrient overview
* ⚠️ Health alerts
* 💡 Personalized insights
* 📋 Active diet plan section

The dashboard is designed to make daily nutrition decisions easier and faster.

The food database is designed around local eating habits instead of only Western packaged foods.

Food detail pages include:

### 🧬 F. Micronutrient Tracking

One of NutriPak’s most important features is micronutrient tracking.

The app looks beyond calories and analyzes nutrients such as:

* 🩸 Iron
* 🦴 Calcium
* ☀️ Vitamin D
* 💊 Vitamin B12
* 🌿 Folate
* 🧪 Zinc
* 🥩 Protein and other nutrition values depending on available food data

This is especially useful because many users may hit calorie targets but still miss important nutrients.

---

### 🧠 G. Nutritional Concerns Analyzer

NutriPak analyzes user-selected nutritional concerns and daily intake patterns to identify possible gaps.

The focus is on food-based improvement.
For example, if a user appears low in a micronutrient, NutriPak aims to suggest relevant foods rather than immediately recommending medicine or supplements.

This makes the app more practical for everyday wellness and safer as an educational nutrition tool.

---

NutriPak includes health-condition-aware nutrition logic.

The app can show warnings and insights based on user health concerns such as:

* 🍬 Diabetes
* 🫀 High blood pressure
* 🧈 Cholesterol
* ⚖️ Obesity
* 🩸 Anemia
* 🌸 PCOS
* 🦋 Thyroid-related concerns
* 🤢 Digestive concerns
* 🫀 Heart, kidney, or liver-related concerns where supported by the app rules

This feature helps users understand when a food may not be ideal for their personal health profile.

NutriPak does not diagnose medical conditions. It provides educational food guidance and encourages users to consult qualified professionals.

### 🤖 I. AI Diet Plan Generation

NutriPak includes AI-assisted diet plan generation powered by Gemini.

The AI diet plan system is designed with safety checks instead of accepting raw AI output blindly.

High-level AI plan features include:

* 🤖 Gemini-powered diet plan generation
* 📅 Day-by-day generation strategy for more reliable structured output
* 🧾 Strict JSON validation
* 🛡️ Final safety validation before saving
* 🕌 Halal-friendly food constraints
* 🚫 Unknown food rejection
* 💊 Medicine and supplement rejection
* 🔥 Calorie target band validation
* 🔁 Retry and backoff handling for rate limits
* 🥘 Use of known food catalog items
* ✅ Final plan validation before user access

The AI system is used as a supportive planning tool, not as a replacement for a registered dietitian or doctor.

## 🤖 AI Diet Plan System

NutriPak’s AI diet planning system uses Gemini to generate structured diet plans while keeping validation as the source of truth.

The system is designed to avoid common AI risks such as malformed responses, unsafe suggestions, unknown foods, excessive calories, or non-food recommendations.

The AI plan system includes:

* 🧾 Structured JSON-based generation
* 📅 Compact day-by-day plan generation
* ✅ Validation before saving
* 🔥 Calorie range checking
* 🕌 Halal-friendly food rules
* 🚫 Rejection of unknown food IDs
* 💊 Rejection of medicine/supplement suggestions
* 🔁 Retry handling for incomplete or rate-limited AI responses
* 🛡️ Final validation of the complete 7-day plan

The goal is not just to generate a diet plan, but to generate a plan that fits the user profile, known food catalog, and safety rules.

## 🏗️ Architecture Overview

NutriPak follows a Clean Architecture-inspired structure with clear separation between UI, business logic, data access, and external services.

### 🎨 Presentation Layer

The presentation layer contains screens, reusable widgets, and GetX controllers. It handles UI state, user interactions, navigation, loading states, and view updates.

### ⚡ Controller Layer

GetX controllers manage screen state and coordinate use cases. Controllers do not directly own the full business logic; they call use cases and repositories where appropriate.

### 🧠 Domain Layer

The domain layer contains entities, repository contracts, and use cases. This keeps business rules independent from Firebase, UI, and platform-specific code.

### 🗄️ Data Layer

The data layer contains models, repository implementations, and local/remote data sources. Firebase and other external services are accessed through this layer.

### 🧩 Services Layer

Services handle cross-cutting integrations such as Firebase initialization, Gemini AI calls, notifications, local storage, and PDF-related functionality.

### 🛡️ Validation Layer

AI-generated plans and health-related outputs are validated before being accepted. This is especially important for safe AI diet planning.
